About

               The University Campus School was started during the academic year 1983-84 to cater to the needs of the children of the employees residing on the campus. The school was originally envisaged to be up to Primary level and run-on self-sufficient basis. Nearly 50% of the students are first generation learners. The campus school provides’ Quality Education’ at nominal fee and extends this facility to the neighbourhood localities as a social obligation.

Presently, with a student strength of 500 and a staff of 30, the school had grown to be a sought-after institution in and around the University Campus.

It’s a great privilege for students who join the school as s Kindergarten Child, can pursue their doctorate from the same reputed University.

The Campus School located on the University Campus has many advantages. The foremost is the very presence of highly educated and morally and intellectually sensitive faculty and an atmosphere of learning and the quest for Knowledge. The enthusiastic students and well-qualified and experienced staff are leaving no stone unturned in living up to the objectives of its establishment.

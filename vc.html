VC's Message

                                                                                                                 

                The University believes that a School must be accessible and affordable to all the sections of society.  Keeping this noble mission in mind and to pave way for excellence in formal education, the University has started the Campus School.  The vision of the School is to provide all-round education, nurture a child's innate curiosity and help the child achieve excellence. 

     To meet this goal, the University maintains:

            - limited class strength - 25 students for KG sections and 30 for primary classes.
            - well-ventilated classrooms with ample natural light and air
            - very friendly, approachable and qualified teachers and staff, and
            - high on hygiene and safety

     Prof. APPA RAO PODILE
